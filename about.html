THE LEGACY OF BOB MARLEY

Celebrating the life, music, and message of a global icon

OUR MISSION

The Bob Marley Official Shop is dedicated to preserving the musical and cultural legacy of Robert Nesta Marley. Through officially licensed merchandise, we celebrate his life while supporting the Bob Marley Foundation's initiatives in education, sustainability, and cultural preservation.

Every purchase helps fund programs that continue Bob's message of love, unity, and social justice around the world.

BOB MARLEY TIMELINE

1945

Birth of a Legend

Robert Nesta Marley was born on February 6 in Nine Mile, Saint Ann Parish, Jamaica.

1963

The Wailers Formed

Bob formed the band "The Wailers" with Peter Tosh and Bunny Wailer, marking the beginning of his musical journey.

1973

International Breakthrough

The Wailers released "Catch a Fire," their first album for Island Records, introducing reggae to a global audience.

1977

Diagnosis & Resilience

Bob was diagnosed with cancer but continued to create music and perform, releasing the iconic "Exodus" album.

1980

Final Performance

Bob's last concert performance was in Pittsburgh, USA, before his health deteriorated.

1981

Passing of an Icon

Bob Marley passed away on May 11 in Miami, Florida, at the age of 36, leaving behind an unparalleled musical legacy.

Today

Living Legacy

Bob's music and message continue to inspire millions worldwide through his recordings, cover versions, and official merchandise.
